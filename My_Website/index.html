About

Hi everyone, my name is Yuan-Jung (David) Lin. This is my personal website which includes almost everything about me. Then, after I graduated from USC, I found that coding skill plays more and more important role in the real world. So I decided to take some times learning coding. The first thing that comes into my mind is building a website. Not only I can let others people know more about me but apply coding skills I learned from on-line courses and textbooks. Website contains my two-year life in USC, bodybuilding, coding diary, and murmur about my worklife.


I am now a Procss Engineer at Lam Research Inc, responsible for the up-to-date thin film deposition technology for Micron Technology and Winbond in Taichung City, Taiwan. While I was in USC, I took lots of semiconductor-related courses such as solid-state processing and integrated circuit laboratory, which fabricated MOSFET in the clean room, and nano-fabrication lithography course which look deeper into the lithography technology through today's industry. Later, I worked in Prof. Wu's group in fab at USC for six months. Finished project using 600nm pitch 1-D grating master mold to fabricate 30nm trench on a wafer using nanoimprint technology and plasma etching. You can find more information about me or connect with me through my linkedin webpage below.
